Lombok to Labuan Bajo Epic Sailing

An incredible 4-day, 3-night maritime expedition starting from our official office at The Brothers Coffee & Resto (Bangsal, Pemenang, North Lombok) heading all the way to Labuan Bajo.

Sail across breathtaking white-sand islands, swim with majestic Whale Sharks at Saleh Bay, hike the iconic Padar Island at sunset, snorkel with manta rays, and stand face-to-face with the legendary Komodo Dragons.

4D3N Sailing Itinerary

Departure Days: Every Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday

Day 1

Lombok Departure & Sunset Trekking

  • Gather and check-in at our basecamp: The Brothers Coffee & Resto in Bangsal, Pemenang.
  • 3-hour scenic drive to Kayangan Harbor in East Lombok (approx. 59 miles) where our vessel awaits.
  • Set sail towards Kambing Island for a light hill trek and capture a stunning island sunset.
Day 2

Whale Shark Phenomenon & Snorkeling

  • Cruise down to the majestic Saleh Bay (8-hour sail) for an unforgettable swim with wild Whale Sharks.
  • Relax, swim, and enjoy pristine snorkeling waters at the beautiful Tambora Beach.
Day 3

Komodo Dragons, Pink Beach & Padar Peak

  • Enter the world-famous Komodo National Park and stand face-to-face with the ancient Komodo Dragons.
  • Swim and sunbathe on the unique, blush-toned sands of Pink Beach.
  • Hike up the dramatic topography of Padar Island for a breathtaking panoramic sunset view.
Day 4

Sea Turtles, Baby Sharks & Labuan Bajo Arrival

  • Snorkel and dive with beautiful wild sea turtles at Manjarita Beach.
  • Hike and spot friendly baby sharks in the crystal-clear shallow waters of Kelor Island.
  • Sail to our final destination, Labuan Bajo, marking the end of our magnificent journey.
